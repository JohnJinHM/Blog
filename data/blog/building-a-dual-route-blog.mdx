The site has two top-level routes, `official` and `casual`, each with its own home, blog, photos, and tags.

A single frontmatter field decides where a post appears:

```yaml
route: official # or 'casual', or 'shared'
```

Posts marked `shared` appear under both routes. Tags are counted per route, while search stays global across everything.
